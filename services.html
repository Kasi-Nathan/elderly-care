Aged Care


At Puthiya Pathai Foundation, we understand the importance of providing comprehensive care for the elderly. Our dedicated team of caregivers is trained to cater to the unique needs of seniors, ensuring their comfort, safety, and happiness. From assistance with daily activities to companionship and emotional support, we aim to make their golden years truly fulfilling.
